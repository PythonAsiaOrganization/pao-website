PythonAsia Online Charity Talk H1 →

The PythonAsia Online Charity Talk Half-One (First Half Of The Year) 2025 is a one-day virtual event that brings together Python developers, engineers, and community advocates from across East and Southeast Asia. Organized by the Python Asia Organization (PAO), this initiative is designed to raise funds for open-source and educational efforts in the region while offering attendees access to expert insights on Python development, tooling, and community leadership.
