Python Asia Organization (PAO) Launches Full-Scale Activities Across East and Southeast Asia

October 2025—The Python Asia Organization (PAO) is excited to announce the launch of its official website, social media channels, vibrant new logo, and an array of dynamic initiatives designed to celebrate and advance the Python programming language throughout East and Southeast Asia. PAO also opens applications for starter sponsorships, creating opportunities for communities to thrive and encouraging meaningful engagement within the Python ecosystem.

The Python Asia Organization (PAO) is a purpose-driven, non-profit entity based in Estonia, dedicated to inspiring growth and innovation in Python programming across East and Southeast Asia. Established to unite Python communities in the region, PAO’s mission is rooted in collaboration, empowerment, and shared success. PAO focuses on:

  • Managing intellectual property rights, organizing, supporting, and providing a robust infrastructure for regional events like our annual regional conference.
  • Supporting programmers and organizers with funding, resources, and mentorship to unlock their full potential.
  • Encouraging collaboration across national boundaries to strengthen regional ties.
  • Streamlining workflows through accessible programs that make organizing easier and more effective.
  • Empowering community leadership with roles and working groups that drive positive change.

PAO proudly unveils its new logo, a bold and thoughtful representation of unity, heritage, and shared prosperity across East, Southeast, and South Asia. The logo features red and gold elements: red signifies energy, celebration, and cultural significance, while gold represents prosperity and the rice harvest. The wavy motif reflects traditional hand-weaving patterns and underscores the interconnectedness of the Python community. This visual identity aligns with PAO’s mission to foster growth, collaboration, and innovation across Asia’s Python programming landscape.

With the launch of its website, social media channels, and refreshed visual identity, PAO is dedicated to advancing its mission of creating a unified, thriving Python programming ecosystem in Asia. The organization aims to empower communities, provide resources, and foster collaboration to ensure that Python programmers across the region have the support they need to succeed.
