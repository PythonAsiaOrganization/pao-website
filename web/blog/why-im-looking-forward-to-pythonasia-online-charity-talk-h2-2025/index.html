Who Is Speaking and What They Will Talk About

Here are the confirmed speakers and what they will focus on:

Audrey M. Roy Greenfeld
Audrey is the CEO of Feldroy and Executive Director of World Impact Lab. She is also known for co-creating Cookiecutter, co-founding PyLadies, and co-organizing the first PyCon Philippines. At this event, she will present Building a Python Web Framework in 2025, exploring what it takes to design and evolve a framework that meets modern developer needs.

Ken Jin
Ken is a Python Core Developer. His talk is Building a New Tail-Calling Interpreter for Python, where he will share how improvements to the interpreter can affect performance, tooling, and future language direction.

Joongi Kim
Joongi is the CTO of Lablup Inc. In his session Enhancing Structured Concurrency with aiotools v2, he will talk about practical ways to manage asynchronous Python code using structured concurrency patterns.

Tomoko Furuki (@komo_fr)
Tomoko is a Python engineer exploring creative applications of AI and hardware. Her talk is LLM Pico Car: How I Built a Voice-Controlled Toy Car Using Generative AI, Gradio, and Raspberry Pi Pico, showing how Python ties together fun hardware projects and large language models.

Daniel Roy Greenfeld
Daniel is co-author of Two Scoops of Django. His talk, also titled Building a Python Web Framework in 2025, will bring another perspective on real-world decisions that go into framework design and maintenance.
